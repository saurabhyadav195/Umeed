How Umeed Works

A simple, transparent process to ensure surplus food reaches those who need it most.

🏢

1. Offer a Donation

Restaurants and food businesses list their surplus food, specifying quantity and expiry.

🤝

2. NGOs Request

Verified NGOs view nearby available food on a map and send a request to claim it.

🚚

3. Coordinated Pickup

Once accepted, both parties communicate to coordinate a swift pickup before the food expires.

🌱

Flexible solutions at scale

Annually, millions of pounds of food is wasted while communities go hungry. Umeed bridges this gap by empowering small donors and large receivers to collaborate seamlessly. All for free.
